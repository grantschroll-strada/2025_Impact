Focus Area Impact

Strada's strategic investments are organized around five focus areas that reflect the foundation's mission to clear the path between education and work. Below are exemplary 2025 impact outcomes from each focus area.

💰
Affordability
Reducing financial barriers to education and improving economic mobility for learners
Summer $78.6M saved

Supported 30,176 users in securing $78.6M in student loan savings and forgiveness by automating enrollment in federal repayment programs — with $61M of that coming through a single partnership with the City of New York.

Clasp 91% employed

91% of borrowers using Clasp's outcome-aligned financing secured employment within 12 months of graduation, earning an average salary of $63.3K — demonstrating that alternative loan structures can expand access while delivering strong learner outcomes.

Rize Education 8,821 students

Registered 8,821 students across 133 small private college partners in high-demand programs like cybersecurity and data analytics — making otherwise costly coursework affordable through a shared program model that lets institutions launch new departments without building from scratch.

🤝
Employer Alignment
Connecting education to employer needs through skills-based hiring and workforce-aligned training
Opus Training 137.5K workers trained

Engaged 137.5K frontline workers in 2025 and facilitated 362K+ skill verifications in over 100 languages. Employers reported a 10% improvement in 90-day retention, and retained workers earned $2.4K more per year through employment stability and promotions.

Julius Education 33.2K jobseekers

Labor market intelligence tools supported 33.2K jobseekers — half of whom were Black or Hispanic — including career mapping for an 11-college Appalachian consortium targeting 1,200 advanced manufacturing placements and talent pipeline development for Oklahoma's $44B aerospace industry.

Skillmaker 8X faster training

Launched an ASE-endorsed VR training platform for skilled trades with 46 automotive training modules. Claims 8X faster training, 80% improvement in knowledge retention, and secured pre-sale commitments from 115 NAPA Auto Parts shops — creating non-degree career onramps aligned directly to employer demand.

🎓
Quality Coaching
Providing personalized guidance, mentorship, and support services that help learners persist and succeed
Mentor Collective 91.4K matched

Matched 91.4K students with mentors across public universities in 2025, with 26% being first-generation students. Mentees reported a 21% increase in comfort seeking professional help and a 42% higher intern-to-full-time conversion rate compared to peers without mentors.

Graduation Alliance 7.4K graduates

Graduated 7.4K learners in 2025 across 180 school districts, with over half completing the flagship Dropout Recovery program. Expanded Adult Workforce Development into five states and helped ~700 New Mexico employees earn high school diplomas through employer partnerships.

Upswing 116K sessions

Brokered 116K tutoring sessions for non-traditional students — 56% at two-year institutions, 88% at public schools. Onboarded Fisk University as its first HBCU partner and added wrap-around mental health and benefits navigation through partnerships with Together All and Single Stop.

⚙️
Work-Based Learning
Creating structured, employer-connected learning experiences that build real skills and career clarity
Riipen 58K learners

Facilitated 55.3K project-based learning experiences across 18K employer relationships in 2025 — and 300K+ since inception. 80% of learners reported clearer career path understanding after completing projects, and a partnership with Lightcast translates learning into market-recognized credentials.

Sol Health 213 therapists trained

Supported 213 therapists-in-training toward licensure while providing affordable therapy to 2.7K users. Users averaged 15 sessions in 2025, and Sol's model directly addresses the nationwide therapist shortage by creating a supervised clinical hours pathway for diverse, early-career clinicians.

📊
Clear Outcomes
Building transparent, verifiable outcomes data so learners, institutions, and employers can make better decisions
Accredible 42M credentials

Issued 42M verifiable digital credentials across 9M users in 2025 — carrying $850M+ in ACE-endorsed college credit value. Introduced a digital transcript feature that tripled sharing rates, making skills and achievements portable and legible to employers and admissions offices.

EdVisorly 504 new admits found

EddyAI's automated transcript processing helped Texas Tech identify 504 additional students eligible for admission — students who would have otherwise been missed. Arcadia University expanded enrollment 109% year-over-year by using EdVisorly's transparent credit evaluation tools.
